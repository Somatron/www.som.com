Pega6 is a career accelerator program for students who want to lead up to Product Management or Software Engineering after High School to prepare for real world jobs.
I was given the fantastic opportunity to be apart of its beta testing program, where I learn the dos and don'ts in the Tech Industry.
I took the role, as a Product Manager, to lead Software developers in creating applications. Like a Calculator or a TODO List

Calculator for Kids

Our first project is a simple online calculator for Kindergarteners. The concept was simple at first, however the more I heard about customer feedback, the more I realized that this Calculator is meant to be designed as an educational learning tool for children to learn about basic Math Operations at the ground level. The visuals are nicely made to appeal to children and a challenging fun mode to help kids solve problems at any pace.

TODO List

Everybody uses a ToDo list, whether it's for school, work, or home life. I've designed the TO DO list as a product manager to make it more effective, visually appealing, and easy to track your progress. It uses size as a way to measure priority, from top being the biggest size and most important priority, to shrinking down as you get to less and less important tasks. It also allows the user to view tasks that they've archived or completed.

AI in a ToDo List

This TO DO list app has included an AI feature where the User can ask the AI to generate new tasks and categories. For example, if a user wants to create a cooking recipe, they can ask the AI to help create several tasks in a new category, showing the ingredients needed, a step-by-step process, and pre-heating the oven.
